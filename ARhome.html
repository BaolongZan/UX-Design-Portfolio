Virtual Decoration

--------- Version 1.0

01. Introduction

The product is focus on shop furniture and decorating houses or rooms in the virtual environment. The users can use the measurement tool to measure the space and items, as well as place the items on virtual environment. They will also be able to save the different decoration project to provide comparison to each other.

02. Concept Ideation

Many people want to decorate their houses or rooms, but when they buy something they don't know what the final effect will be. The AR product I designed can decorate the house through a virtual environment.

03. Problem Statement

" Homeowners need to find furniture and decorations that match their existing decor and meet their standards for beauty, functionality, and affordability because they struggle to balance aesthetics, practicality, and cost when selecting new items for their home. "

04. Persona

Li Zhang

Full-time Housewife

Beijing, China

38 years olde

Married, 2 child

“I need tools to better control costs while finding furniture that matches my home's style and meets my standards for quality and design.”

Hobby:

Gardening, interior design, cooking habit of shopping: Focus on quality and design, willing to pay extra for unique and high-quality home products.

Motivation:

Wei Zhang has a strong interest in home decoration. She often redecorates various rooms in her home in pursuit of a perfect and harmonious home atmosphere. She hopes to use AR software to experiment with different decorative styles and furniture layouts to achieve the best visual effects.

05. Brainstorm

AR measure tool

"Uses AR technology to measure room size and ensure furniture fits, solving the issue of unsuitable furniture sizes and providing a fun interactive experience."

Cost Estimates

"The product offers real-time cost estimation, allowing users to see the total price of their furniture and decor selections, helping them stay within budget and avoid overspending."

Decorating Simulator

"Users can place furniture and decorations in a virtual room using AR technology, helping them make better purchasing decisions."

Projects Comparison

"Allows users to save snapshots of different designs and easily compare past and present designs, aiding in their final decision-making."

06. AR implementation

Spatial measurement tools

Provides an AR spatial measurement tool that allows users to quickly and accurately determine if the furniture size is suitable for the room.

Real-time cost estimates

Display the price of each item along with the total price to allow users to control their budget.

Virtual furniture implementation

Allow users to use AR technology to place furniture and decorations in their actual living spaces, enabling them to see the effects of different layouts and styles.

Visual historical comparison function

Allow users to save their designs, enabling them to compare various designs, including aspects such as location and pattern matching.

07. User Journey (Concise)

08. Wireframe

Users can use their cellphone camera to place virtual furniture and decorations in their virtual environment. They can then select, copy, rotate, move, and delete these items. The app also includes a function to display the size of the items, measure objects, save current design projects, and view the history of design projects.

09. Experience Journey

1. Select the category of furniture you like on the market page in the virtual environment.

2. Select an item of furniture.

3. Click the add button please it in the virtual environment.

4. Be able to rotate or move the item.

5. View all of the items in the cart on the pay page. Include total price.
